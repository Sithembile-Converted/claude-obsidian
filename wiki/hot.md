## Key Recent Facts

- mTOR (mTORC1) is the master molecular switch for muscle protein synthesis — activated by mechanical load + leucine
- Sleep is the most anabolic variable: 70% of GH pulses during deep sleep; sleep deprivation cuts testosterone 24%
- Evidence-based training: 10-20 sets/muscle/week, 8-12 reps (any range near failure works), 2+ days/week per muscle
- Protein target: 1.6-2.2g/kg/day, distributed 20-40g every 3-4 hours
- Calorie surplus: 200-300 cal above maintenance (lean bulk beats aggressive bulk)
- Post-workout window: within 1-2 hours, 20-30g protein + fast carbs
- Pre-sleep casein (cottage cheese): feeds overnight MPS during 7-9 hrs of sleep
